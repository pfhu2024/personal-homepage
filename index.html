Bio

Born in Columbus, Ohio in 2014, Pippi began studying the violin at an age eight, in 2022, quickly distinguishing himself from his peers through his musical sensitivity and natural command of the instrument. In 2023, one year after he started his journey, he received the Honorable Mention prize in the Elementary Division of the Dallas Music Teachers Association Competition. The following year, he was awarded 1st Prize in the same division, reflecting his continued artistic growth and dedication. Pippi has studied with distinguished and respected pedagogues Ms. Rhea Edelman, Ms. Nicolette Solomon, Ms. Julia Bushkova, Ms. Dina Nesterenko, and Mr. Simon James, in chronological order. He currently studies with Ms. Dina Nesterenko and Mr. Simon James. Under their guidance, he has developed a strong technical foundation, refined tonal production, and an increasingly mature interpretive voice. Committed to disciplined practice and artistic excellence, he approaches his studies with focus, curiosity, and determination. As he continues to expand his repertoire and musical understanding, he aspires to deepen both his technical mastery and expressive range. Beyond his musical pursuits, he enjoys origami, math, and other creative activities, which reflect the same patience, precision, and thoughtful craftsmanship that characterize his violin playing.
